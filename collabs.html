Collaborations

Rímnadans

2024

Participatory performance work by the Icelandic composer collective S.L.Á.T.U.R., inspired by traditional Icelandic rímur chanting and dance practices. Premiered at Harpa during Dark Music Days 2024.

Phygital Glitch

2022

Music and sound for the performance work by choreographer and artist Gígja Jónsdóttir, created in collaboration with graduating students of the contemporary dance department of the Iceland University of the Arts.

Oneirokritikon

2023

Music by sideproject for the short film Oneirokritikon by visual artist Arna Beth, exhibited at Hafnarborg in the exhibition "Landscape for the Chosen One."

Töfrafundur áratug síðar

2021

Performance work by Libia Castro & Ólafur Ólafsson. Musician/performer role at Reykjavík Art Museum in collaboration with Cycle Art Festival.

Sigtryggur Berg: A Compendium Of Terrestrial Disjection — Release Party

2023

Performance with the band Glupsk at Posthúsið as part of Sequences Art Festival.

Arto Lindsay — I Had A Fever When

2023

In 2023 Arto Lindsay came to Iceland to play in Mengi for two gigs. I ended up doing sound on one of the gigs. For the trip he also wanted to make an album which I helped record, and ended up contributing electronics. The session also included Skúli Sverrisson and John McCowen.
